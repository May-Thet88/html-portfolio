The Best Movie According to May Thet

My top three movies of all time

1. Me before you

A romantic movie that will make you end up crying buckets. That is the only movie that made me preoccupied with the sad ending. I was really choked up.

2. Marry My husband

Marry my husband is a K-drama, which genre is not my cup of tea. Inspite of a revenge movie, it is also a great film that is capable of catching my attention.
A pratagonist go back time in ten years and work very hard to pay her husband and best friend back for what they've done.

3.Mean Girls

I'm not in the habit of re-watching movies that I've ever watched. But this one that features teenager's lives is beyond amazing. Hghly recommend if you're finding a feel-good movie.
